Follow your dream

  • Research about the course and apply to the college
    Every journey starts with a single thought, ponder enough and everything becomes clear. Putting enough thought and research will make sure that you make the right choice and take the right path. Applying for dream college and program is one thing, keeping a plan B is important to make sure that your dream of studying abroad is achieved. Check the application date and try to apply on the first day. Make sure you have your SOP ready so you don't waste your time.
    aleast 12 months before the intake
  • Give IELTS exam
    Academic IELTS is required for most colleges as an assurance that student will understand lectures imparted in English language. ILTES score is also required for Visa.
    atleast 9 months before the intake
  • Funding the education
    Not all of us are blessed with parents who have deep pockets. These days taking student loan is not as difficult as it once was. Make sure to apply for loan as soon as you get the college admission letter as loan approval can take 2-3 months.
    after the admission letter

  • GIC
    GIC is mandatory requirement for Visa. GIC can be included in the education loan amount. Keep all the document GIC document safe as you would need them for visa application
    Before the visa application
  • Medical Exam
    Medical exam is the last step before applying visa. Make sure you pass the medical exam. Check with your medical center if you require to give any more test. Medical exam can be taken any time so don't wait and give you medical test early. Remember, medical result is valid for 1 year.
    Before 5 months of intake
  • Apply for Visa
    Apply for visa at least 3 months prior to give yourself time to get cheap flights tickets. Moreover, for some reason if visa gets rejected first time, you will have time to apply for it again.
    atleast 3 months prior to intake
